Izuku Midoriya

Izuku "Deku" Midoriya is the main protagonist of the manga and anime My Hero Academia.

Short biography

Full name: Izuku Midoriya

Age: 15 (before entering UA)

Floor:Male

Race: A person with a quirk

Occupation: Yuei Academy student

Powers: Quirk "One for All"

Goals: Become a professional superhero Defeat the League of Villains

Appearance

Izuku Midoriya is a boy of average height. His hair is dark green. The superhero costume is a green jumpsuit with black knee and elbow pads, a red belt and shoes of the same color, as well as white gloves.

Origin and characteristics

Deku is an ordinary schoolboy who did not have quirks, but later received one from All Might and entered the U.A. Academy. He is friendly by nature and even treats his enemies with respect. Izuku Midoriya is a master strategist. It is not clear how, but in times of danger his strategic thinking runs wild.

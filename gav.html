Interesting Facts

During the early stages of creation, Izuku was known as Mikumo Akatani. He had a long curly hairstyle and his bangs covered his eyes. He was also quirkless, relying more on homemade gadgets and luck to overcome obstacles. His hero costume was a little more intimidating. However, Horikoshi, the manga's author, noted that he was unhappy with Mikumo's design, especially his hair. This forced the mangaka to come up with the current design with a slightly shorter hairstyle.

Izuku's name contains the kanji for "green" (緑), "valley" (谷), "to come from/be from a certain place" (出), and "long ago" (久). "久" is also a homophone of "nine" (九 is usually read as Kyu), as he is the ninth user of One For All.

"Deku" (でく literally means "one who cannot achieve/do something") is another reading of his name Izuku (出久). The derisive nickname "Deku" was given to him by Katsuki in early childhood.It is Ochako who finds the nickname inspiring because she sees "Deku" as an "I can do this" (頑張るでく) message.

Izuku's first hero costume cost ¥40,380 (~23515.33 rubles)

Regarding school life and Izuku's achievements at U.A.: He is the 18th student in class 1-A. Took 7th place in the entrance exam. Placed last in the initial Quirk Understanding test. Took 4th place among 1-A class in the semi-annual test. Took 8th place at the Yuei Sports Festival among first-year students.

Popularity poll rankings look like this: Ranked 1st in the First Popularity Poll. Ranked 2nd in the Second, Third and Fifth popularity polls. Ranked 3rd in the Fourth Popularity Poll. Izuku's favorite dish is Katsudon. Izuku was intended to be an adult character, as seen in the My Hero Academia one-shot. Shares his birthday with the character Ichigo Kurosaki from the manga Bleach, another manga in Weekly Shōnen Jump. He also shares his birthday with another U.A. Academy student, Mirio Togata. Izuku is similar to the manga character Hunter x Hunter Gon Freecss. Both have dark hair with a green tint. Both have basic abilities that include increased strength. Both harm themselves with their own abilities. Izuku also bears some resemblance to the main character of the film Aerobatics, Will Stronghold, who also initially did not have any superpowers. Izuku's hero costume can be purchased at a Japanese supermarket for 2,427 yen. His zodiac sign is Cancer.

General Abilities

Combat Skills: Izuku has decent fighting skills and physical attributes above the average student. However, he improved his physical capabilities through training, which was enough to provide valuable assistance during his battle with the Stain. He usually relied on his fists to attack, however he decided to change his fighting style based on his kicks, which are stronger than his arms.

Keen Mind: Izuku has proven to be extremely smart on many occasions. He showed deep observation and analysis skills. Typically, his intelligence helped him more than his Quirk. Izuku is able to formulate tactics to gain an advantage in a situation and uses his knowledge to creatively utilize other people's Quirks to their fullest, such as working with Tsuyu and Minoru to escape with them from the villains in the flood zone in the U.S.J.. Izuku also has extensive knowledge about Professional Heroes and their Quirks. Thanks to Izuku's intelligence, he figured out the mechanics of One For All, allowing him to use his Quirk more effectively in a short period of time. Gran Torino even praised Izuku's intelligence, saying that Izuku is a person who thinks before he acts.

High Pain Threshold: On many occasions, Izuku has demonstrated that he can tolerate large amounts of pain. This is mainly due to Quirk abuse; however, he was able to move despite feeling excruciating pain.

Fighting techniques

  • Detroit Punch (デトロイトスマッシュ Detoroito Sumasshu): The same move as All Might's, but performed by Izuku as an uppercut.
  • Delaware Punch (デラウエアスマッュ Derauea Sumasshu): Izuku makes a "snap" with his fingers, creating a shock wave. The punch comes out so strong that Izuku's fingers break, due to which he cannot use this move more than 10 times in a fight. However, in emergency cases, he can perform this technique even with broken fingers, causing severe injuries to them.
  • One For All: Full Coverage (ワン・フォー・オール フルカウル Wan Fō Ōru Furukauru): Izuku passes a certain percentage of One For All's power through his body on a permanent basis rather than momentarily like the moves above, allowing him to last longer fight and don't break your bones. This method of using his quirk gives him greater agility. Activating this technique grants him increased strength, speed, mobility, and agility.
  • One For All 100%: A technique that allows Izuku to attack using the full power of his quirk in the desired part of his body, followed by severe damage to it. Continued use of the same limb after one such attack can cause fractures, as seen in his fights against Shoto Todoroki (resulting in Izuku having surgery on his right arm) and muscle tears (resulting in several scars on his right arm).
  • One For All: Full Cover - Side Style (ワンフォーオール フルカウル シュートスタイル Wan Fō Ōru Furu Kauru Shūto Sutairu): By activating One For All Full Cover, Izuku uses a fighting style focused on kicks, which are naturally stronger than arms.
  • One For All: Full Coverage - 20%: Midoriya uses 20% of the full power of his quirk and distributes it throughout his body, gaining an even greater boost in power and speed. It doesn't break his bones, but it does put quite a bit of strain on his body.
  • One For All: Full Coverage - 30%: Midoriya uses 30% of the full power of his quirk and distributes it throughout his body, gaining an even greater boost in power and speed. Midoriya was able to use this technique near the end of his first year at UA, during the war with the Paranormal Liberation Front.
  • One For All: Full Coverage - 100% (ワン・フォー・オール フルカウル Wan Fō Ōru Furu Kauru 100 Pāsento): Izuku activates his Quirk at full power, covering his entire body, gaining a huge increase in speed and strength, allowing him to easily overwhelm really strong enemies. This technique was first used to protect Eri. The increase in power appears to give him fire trails emanating from his eyes. Being in direct contact with Eri, Midoriya is able to be in this mode without harming his body at all. Since Eri has the ability to "rewind" a person's body condition, any damage done to Izuku's body when using One For All will be 100% negated by Eri's Quirk, which proved useful in his fight against the villain.
  • Froppy Style (フロッピースタイル Furoppīsutairu): Using a black whip, Izuku releases a "tendril" from his mouth and uses it in the same way Tsuyu does with his tongue.
  • Full Shot (全ぜん開かい Zenkai): Using a smoke screen, Izuku activates it with maximum power to instantly release a large amount of smoke.
  • Spot Focus (一いっ点てん特とっ化か Itten Tokka): Using a black whip, it quickly fires a "tendril" at a specific target to attack them.
  • Artificial 100 Percent (疑ぎ似じ 100%パーセント Giji Hyaku-pāsento): Izuku uses 45% One For All, and stores kinetic energy with Fa Jin. By doing this, he can achieve the blinding speed of using One For All at 100%, without the accompanying recoil.
  • Black Chain (黒こく鎖さ Kokusa): By combining the Black Whip and Fa Jin, Izuku can create a chain that he can use to bind his opponent.
  • Transmission (トランスミッション Toransumisshon): Izuku rushes towards his opponent and strikes them multiple times, with each strike increasing the speed shift gear affecting his opponent.
  • Overdrive (五速オーバードライブ Ōbādoraibu): Izuku uses Speed Shift and Fa Jin on himself, increasing his speed and strength to catch up with his opponent and unleash powerful attacks on them. The power he unleashes is described as 120% One For All.

Quirks and Abilities

Quirks

One For All (ワン・フォー・オール Wan Fō Ōru): Izuku's quirk, which he received from All Might. This Quirk gives him superhuman strength, agility, and mobility by channeling power through his body. Thanks to this Quirk, Izuku increases his physical abilities (strength, speed, endurance and agility) to superhuman levels.

In the early days of using One For All, Izuku caused enormous damage to himself, including breaking his limbs. Recently, Izuku's reflexes and speed have improved significantly, allowing him to hold his own against particularly strong opponents for longer. Izuku can focus his accumulated power in one part of his body or distribute it evenly throughout his body, although concentrating the power in one part of the body causes a huge strain, causing him considerable pain. Currently, Midoriya can control 45% of his Quirk's full power without breaking limbs, but his normal output is 30%.

The "core" of One For All, which was hidden behind the physical strength accumulated over several generations, contains the quirks of its past owners. This gives Izuku the opportunity to use the quirks of past owners of One For All. He can even use multiple learned quirks at the same time, giving him even more options in battle. Known One For All Quirks Midoriya has gained so far:

  • Speed Switch (変へん速そく Hensoku): A quirk belonging to the second owner of One For All. This Quirk allows one to change the speed of an object the wearer touches.
  • Fa Jin (発はっ勁けい Hakkei): A quirk that belonged to the third owner of One For All. This Quirk allows Izuku to store and create kinetic energy while performing movements, which subsequently allows him to use the accumulated energy in an explosive burst of speed and power.
  • Premonition of Danger (危き機き感かん知ち Kiki kanchi): A quirk belonging to Hikage Shinomori, the fourth owner of One For All. The Quirk allows its user to sense threats in their surrounding area. Izuku describes its effect as a stabbing feeling in the head.
  • Black Whip (黒鞭クロムチ Kuro Muchi): A quirk owned by Daigoro Banjo, the fifth wielder of One For All. Allows you to create whips of dark energy and use them to grab objects at long distances, as well as pull the user towards these objects.
  • Smokescreen (煙えん幕まく Enmaku): A quirk belonging to En, the sixth owner of One For All. Allows Izuku to create a thick cloud of smoke from his body. This smoke can cover a very large area and obscure the view of opponents.
  • Flight (浮ふ遊ゆう Fuyū): A quirk owned by Nana Shimura, the seventh owner of One For All. This quirk allows Izuku to maintain his position and move in the air.
